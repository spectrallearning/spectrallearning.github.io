Call for papers

We invite submissions of papers related to method of moments / spectral methods in machine learning. We strongly support a broad definition of spectral learning as any method for learning or fitting a model based on a spectral decomposition of observable data.

Extended abstracts should be submitted using the ICML 2014 format with a maximum of 4 pages (not including references). Please e-mail your submission to workshop.spectral.learning@gmail.com with the subject line "Submission to Workshop on Method of Moments and Spectral Learning".

Concurrent submissions to the workshop and the main conference (or other conferences) are most welcome.

Important dates

  • Submission deadline: April 10, 2014
  • Notification of acceptance: April 21, 2014
  • Workshop: June 25 or 26, 2014
